MEET OUR TEAM

Negovan "Luka" Petkovic

Luka founded Rockville Remodeling in 2005. A mason’s son, Luka grew up building and working on houses in his native Serbia, where he later trained as a civil engineer. Over the past decade in the United States, he has worked with virtually every aspect of residential property construction, and the gamut of materials used to build and remodel them. In addition to home improvement licenses held in Maryland and DC, he has a First Category Stationary Engineering License (for work with pumps, generators, boilers, etc). Luka will work with you every step of the way to achieve a remodel that perfectly suits your needs, your lifestyle and your budget.

Miso Novakovic

Miso joined Rockville Remodeling in early 2011. Recently relocated from Chicago, Miso is the former owner of a commercial interior remodeling business with past clients including Panera Bread and Starbucks. He brings more than 20 years experience with all kinds of remodeling jobs and, he performs much of our projects’ labor himself. Miso is Luka’s right-hand man. He oversees and inspects the work of all subcontractors (when they are required) to make sure it meets Rockville Remodeling’s standards of quality.
